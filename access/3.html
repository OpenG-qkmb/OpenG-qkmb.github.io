Empty child

He can help people killed by TGT kids to heal.


Name: Empty child

Age: 14

Haer GrC: B

Haer Code: 3

Info:
When a person is killed by a TGT kid, he will come to help the person. But because of his limited ability, only one person can be cured in a week. He secretes a fluid from his head, mixes it with another of his body fluids and applies it to the wounds of the deceased. The deceased can heal the wound and resume normal life.
